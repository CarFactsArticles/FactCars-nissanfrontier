FactCars: The Nissan Frontier

Overview

The Nissan Frontier is a pickup truck that was first produced in 1997. Some of the models include the S, SV, PRO-X, PRO-4X, and SL. The MSRP for a 2025 Nissan Frontier is about $32,050 and it has a 3.8 L V6 Engine and has 310 Horsepower.

Differences Between the Models

Entry Level Trims

  1. S - The entry level trim

  2. SV - An upgrade from the S, offering more features
    and available in King Cab, Crew Cab, and Crew Cab Long Bed models.

    Off-Roading focused trims

  1. PRO-X: A more off-road-capable trim with features like all-terrain
    tires and Bilstein off-road shocks.

  2. PRO-4X: An even more robust off-road trim with
    four-wheel drive, electronic locking differential, and heavy-duty skid plates

Luxury

  1. SL: The highest trim level, focusing on comfort and convenience with
    features like remote engine start, a Fender audio system, and more

Summary

The Nissan Frontier is a rugged and reliable mid-size pickup truck designed for both work and adventure. It features a powerful 3.8L V6 engine delivering 310 horsepower, paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission for smooth performance. The Frontier offers impressive towing capabilities of up to 7,150 lbs and a payload capacity of 1,610 lbs, making it a versatile choice for heavy-duty tasks. Inside, it boasts modern technology like a 12.3-inch touchscreen with wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, along with advanced safety features such as automatic emergency braking and pedestrian detection. With its off-road-ready 4x4 options and durable construction, the Frontier is built to handle tough terrains while providing comfort and convenience for everyday use.

The International Nissan Frontier - The Nissan Narvana

The Nissan Navara is a durable and versatile pickup truck that balances power, safety, and comfort. It features robust engines like the 2.3L twin-turbo diesel, advanced safety systems such as automatic emergency braking and a 360-degree camera, and exceptional off-road capabilities with 4WD, hill descent control, and rear differential lock. Inside, it offers a spacious cabin and tech like an 8-inch touchscreen supporting Android Auto and Apple CarPlay. With a towing capacity of up to 3,500 kg and a payload capacity exceeding 1,100 kg, it's ideal for both work and leisure.
